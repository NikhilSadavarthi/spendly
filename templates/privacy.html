Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal information such as your email address and password when you create an account. Additionally, the service collects financial transaction data (expenses, categories, dates, and amounts) that you voluntarily enter into the application.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve the expense tracking features of Spendly. This includes displaying financial summaries, statistics, and historical logs of your expenses. We do not use your data for advertising, nor do we sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our databases. We take industry-standard measures to protect your personal and financial information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your financial information with third-party services. In the future, if third-party integrations (such as cloud backups or bank connections) are introduced, we will seek your explicit consent before transmitting any data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example.com.
